Visiting Professor of Aeronautics

Professor Sheila Widnall from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology will be delivering lectures on the development of aeronautics and where the future lies in this exciting "space".

Get your family together for an exciting night in the museum as you sleep over beside dinosaurs and science displays. Bring your own sleeping bag and get ready to go exploring the wonders of science.
